Cooking Tips

Essential techniques from traditional Chinese cuisine, adapted for home kitchens worldwide.

01

The Wok's Secret

High heat is essential. Let your wok smoking hot before adding oil — this creates the signature "wok hei" flavor.

02

Mise en Place

Chinese cooking moves fast. Prepare all ingredients before heating the wok. Everything should be cut, measured, and within arm's reach.

03

Marinating Magic

Even 15 minutes of marinating with soy sauce and cornstarch transforms ordinary chicken into something tender.
