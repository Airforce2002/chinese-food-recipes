Our Story

Bringing Authentic Chinese Cooking to Every Kitchen

We're on a mission to demystify Chinese cuisine and make traditional recipes accessible to home cooks everywhere.

The Beginning

From Grandma's Kitchen to the World

Wok & Roll started in 2020 when our founder, raised on her grandmother's Sichuan cooking, realized that most Chinese recipe sites either oversimplified dishes or assumed access to ingredients no one could find.

We set out to bridge that gap — creating recipes that respect tradition while acknowledging the reality of home kitchens around the world. Every recipe is tested multiple times, adjusted for standard home equipment, and written with clear instructions.

"Good Chinese cooking isn't about fancy techniques. It's about understanding heat, timing, and balance."

Our recipes come from documented family traditions, verified against regional culinary schools, and refined through feedback from thousands of home cooks across 40+ countries.

Our Mission

To preserve and share authentic Chinese culinary traditions while making them approachable for modern home cooks.

🌏

Global Accessibility

Recipes work with ingredients available worldwide. We provide substitutions and alternatives for hard-to-find items.

📚

Cultural Preservation

🔥

Practical Techniques

Every method is adapted for home kitchens. No restaurant equipment required — just passion and a good wok.

What We Believe

🔍

Verification Over Assumption

Every recipe is tested extensively. We don't publish untested recipes or pass along culinary myths without verification.

🤝

Respect for Tradition

Chinese cooking has thousands of years of history. We honor traditional methods while making them work at home.

✨

Clarity Over Showmanship

No unnecessary jargon. Clear instructions, honest timing, and straightforward language. Your success is our measure.

🌱

Sustainability Matters

We encourage local sourcing, seasonal eating, and mindful ingredient choices. Great cooking can be responsible cooking.
